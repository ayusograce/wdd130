Ecuador

Ecuador is a latinamerican country full of natural riches and turistic sites. It is bordered by Colombia to the north, Peru to the east and south, and the Pacific Ocean to the west. It has four regions: a tropical, a mountain, a jungle, and an island region. Quito es the capital of Ecuador and one of the most poblated cities in the country. Overall, Ecuador is a country with a variety of things to do and places to visit!
